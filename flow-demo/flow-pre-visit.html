Flow 04 of 15

Pre-Visit Questionnaire & Check-In

Completing intake forms and health questionnaires digitally before arriving — streamlining the check-in process and giving providers advance context.

Notifications Forms Medications Insurance Check-In Allergies
1
Receive Pre-Visit Notification
Notifications
▾

Three days before the appointment, the patient receives a notification with a link to complete pre-visit forms. The system sends reminders at 3 days, 1 day, and 2 hours before.

How This Helps
  • Tiered reminder cadence maximizes form completion rates
  • Direct deep links take patients straight to the forms
  • Mobile-optimized notifications work on any device
2
Complete Health Questionnaire
Forms
▾

The patient fills out a specialty-specific questionnaire — for a dermatology visit, questions about skin conditions, medications, allergies, and family history of skin cancer. Smart branching adapts questions based on answers.

How This Helps
  • Specialty-specific forms capture clinically relevant information
  • Smart branching eliminates irrelevant questions
  • Auto-populated fields from existing records save patient time
3
Update Medications & Allergies
Medications
▾

The patient reviews their current medication list and updates it — adding a new allergy to penicillin and confirming current dosages. The system checks for drug interactions automatically.

How This Helps
  • Drug interaction checking catches potential dangers before the visit
  • Visual medication list makes review quick and easy
  • Allergy updates propagate to all providers in the network
4
Upload Insurance Card
Insurance
▾

The patient photographs both sides of their updated insurance card. OCR verifies the information matches their profile and flags any discrepancies for review.

How This Helps
  • Pre-visit card upload eliminates front desk scanning delays
  • OCR validation catches card photo quality issues early
  • Updated insurance info prevents claim denials
5
Digital Check-In
Check-In
▾

On the day of the appointment, the patient checks in digitally from the parking lot. The system confirms arrival, estimates wait time, and sends a notification when the provider is ready.

How This Helps
  • Contactless check-in reduces waiting room crowding
  • Real-time wait time estimates reduce patient anxiety
  • Provider notification when patient arrives optimizes clinic flow
