Flow 10 of 15

Bill Review & Online Payment

Transparent billing with itemized statements, insurance claim tracking, cost estimates, and flexible online payment options.

Billing Statements Payments Insurance Payment Plans Receipts
1
Statement Notification
Billing
▾

The patient receives a notification that a new statement is available. The notification shows the total amount due and payment due date without revealing service details on the lock screen.

How This Helps
  • Timely notification prevents missed payment deadlines
  • HIPAA-safe preview protects billing privacy
  • Direct link to payment page reduces friction
2
Itemized Statement Review
Statements
▾

The patient reviews the itemized statement showing each service, the insurance adjustment, what the plan paid, and the remaining patient responsibility. CPT codes are translated to plain-language descriptions.

How This Helps
  • Itemized detail builds trust and transparency
  • Plain-language descriptions help patients understand charges
  • Insurance vs. patient responsibility breakdown prevents confusion
3
Insurance Claim Tracking
Insurance
▾

The patient tracks their insurance claim status — submitted, processing, adjudicated, or denied. For denied claims, the system explains the reason and suggests next steps (appeal, contact insurance).

How This Helps
  • Claim status visibility reduces billing inquiry calls
  • Denial reason explanations empower patients to take action
  • Appeal templates help patients contest denied claims
4
Online Payment
Payments
▾

The patient pays the $45 copay online using a stored credit card. The system offers payment plan options for larger balances — splitting into 3, 6, or 12 monthly installments with no interest.

How This Helps
  • Stored payment methods enable one-click payments
  • Payment plans reduce bad debt and improve collection rates
  • Multiple payment methods (card, bank, HSA) serve all patients
5
Receipt & Payment History
Billing
▾

A receipt is generated immediately and the patient's payment history is updated. The system shows year-to-date spending, remaining deductible, and out-of-pocket maximum progress.

How This Helps
  • Instant receipts serve as proof of payment for tax purposes
  • Deductible tracking helps patients plan healthcare spending
  • Year-to-date summary supports HSA/FSA management
