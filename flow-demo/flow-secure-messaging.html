Flow 09 of 15

Secure Messaging with Care Team

HIPAA-compliant messaging between patients and their care team — replacing phone tag with threaded, trackable, and documented conversations.

Messaging Care Team Attachments Triage Scheduling Notifications
1
Compose New Message
Messaging
▾

The patient starts a new message to their care team about a concerning mole. They select the message category (medical question, prescription request, scheduling, billing) which routes to the right team member.

How This Helps
  • Category-based routing ensures messages reach the right person
  • Subject line templates guide patients to provide relevant details
  • Draft auto-save prevents message loss
2
Attach Photos & Documents
Messaging
▾

The patient attaches a photo of the mole from their phone camera. The system supports image, PDF, and document attachments up to 25MB with automatic virus scanning.

How This Helps
  • Photo attachments enable visual communication between visits
  • Virus scanning protects the healthcare network
  • Document support allows sharing of external records
3
Triage & Routing
Care Team
▾

The message is triaged by the care team's nurse. Based on the photo, she escalates it to the dermatologist with an urgent flag. The patient sees the status update: "Escalated to Dr. Smith."

How This Helps
  • Nurse triage filters and prioritizes incoming messages
  • Escalation paths ensure urgent issues reach specialists quickly
  • Status visibility keeps patients informed without calling
4
Provider Response
Messaging
▾

Dr. Smith reviews the photo and responds within 4 hours: "This looks like it should be evaluated in person. I've asked my scheduler to find you an appointment this week." A scheduling link is included.

How This Helps
  • Photo-based assessment prevents unnecessary ER visits
  • Embedded scheduling links reduce friction for follow-up
  • Response time tracking ensures SLA compliance
5
Message Thread & History
Messaging
▾

The full conversation thread is preserved and linked to the patient's chart. Previous message threads are searchable and organized by date and provider.

How This Helps
  • Threaded conversations maintain context across exchanges
  • Chart-linked messages create a documentation trail
  • Search functionality helps patients find past advice
