Profile Setup & Family Linking

Setting up a complete patient profile with demographics, preferences, and family account linking for managing dependents and shared health information.

Profile Insurance Preferences Family Linking Health Records Demographics
1
Complete Demographics
Profile
▾

The patient fills in detailed demographics — address, preferred language, race/ethnicity, and communication preferences. The system auto-suggests addresses and validates postal codes.

How This Helps
  • Auto-suggest reduces data entry errors and speeds completion
  • Language preference ensures communications arrive in the right language
  • Demographics data supports population health analytics
2
Insurance Information
Insurance
▾

The patient adds insurance details by photographing their insurance card. OCR extracts member ID, group number, and plan information automatically. Manual entry is also available.

How This Helps
  • OCR card scanning reduces manual data entry by 80%
  • Real-time eligibility checks confirm coverage immediately
  • Multiple insurance plans can be stored for coordination of benefits
3
Communication Preferences
Preferences
▾

The patient sets notification preferences — email, SMS, or push for appointment reminders, lab results, billing alerts, and health tips. Frequency and timing preferences are configurable.

How This Helps
  • Granular notification control reduces alert fatigue
  • Preferred channel delivery increases message open rates
  • Quiet hours respect patient lifestyle and time zones
4
Family Account Linking
Family Linking
▾

A parent links their children's accounts for proxy access. The system verifies the relationship through date of birth matching and sends a confirmation to the linked account (for adults).

How This Helps
  • Proxy access lets parents manage children's healthcare in one place
  • Relationship verification prevents unauthorized account linking
  • Age-based access rules automatically adjust as children become adults
5
Health History Import
Health Records
▾

The patient imports health records from previous providers through a secure FHIR-based data exchange. Allergies, medications, immunizations, and conditions are automatically structured.

How This Helps
  • FHIR-based import eliminates manual record re-entry
  • Structured data enables clinical decision support
  • Historical records give providers complete patient context
