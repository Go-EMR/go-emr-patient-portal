Health Record Download & Sharing

Accessing, downloading, and securely sharing complete health records — supporting patient data portability and care coordination across providers.

Health Records Record Export Record Sharing FHIR Audit Trail Data Portability
1
Access Health Record Summary
Health Records
▾

The patient navigates to their health records dashboard showing a comprehensive timeline — encounters, diagnoses, medications, allergies, immunizations, labs, and vitals organized chronologically.

How This Helps
  • Unified timeline gives a complete health picture at a glance
  • Chronological organization reveals patterns over time
  • Category filters help find specific record types quickly
2
Download Records
Record Export
▾

The patient requests a download of their complete health record. The system generates a CCD (Continuity of Care Document) in both human-readable PDF and machine-readable FHIR format.

How This Helps
  • PDF format serves insurance, legal, and personal record needs
  • FHIR export enables interoperability with other health systems
  • Date range selection limits exports to relevant timeframes
3
Share with Another Provider
Record Sharing
▾

Moving to a new city, the patient shares their records with a new primary care doctor. A secure sharing link is generated with the recipient's email verification and configurable access duration.

How This Helps
  • Email-verified sharing ensures only intended recipients access records
  • Configurable access duration maintains patient control
  • Share history shows who has accessed what and when
4
Revoke Access
Record Sharing
▾

The patient reviews their active sharing links and revokes access for a previous specialist who no longer needs records. The revocation is immediate and logged.

How This Helps
  • One-click revocation gives patients control over their data
  • Access logs provide transparency about who viewed records
  • Immediate revocation prevents further access after relationship ends
5
Data Portability Request
Record Export
▾

Under patient data rights, the patient requests a complete data export including all visit notes, lab results, imaging reports, and billing history. The export is prepared within 24 hours.

How This Helps
  • Regulatory compliance with 21st Century Cures Act information blocking rules
  • Complete export includes all data categories
  • Notification when export is ready for download
