Allergy & Medication Interaction Alerts

Proactive safety alerts for drug-drug interactions, drug-allergy conflicts, and medication safety checks across the patient's complete medication profile.

Allergies Medications Notifications Messaging Drug Interactions Safety Alerts
1
Allergy Profile Update
Allergies
▾

The patient adds a new allergy to sulfa drugs after an adverse reaction. The system captures the reaction type (rash, anaphylaxis, GI upset), severity, and onset timing.

How This Helps
  • Structured allergy data enables precise clinical decision support
  • Severity tracking differentiates true allergies from intolerances
  • Onset timing helps providers assess causality
2
Cross-Reference with Medications
Medications
▾

The system immediately cross-references the new sulfa allergy against the patient's active medications. A conflict is detected: the patient is taking sulfasalazine, which contains a sulfonamide component.

How This Helps
  • Automatic cross-referencing catches conflicts the patient might not recognize
  • Active medication scanning covers all current prescriptions
  • Ingredient-level checking catches indirect allergy conflicts
3
Alert to Patient & Provider
Notifications
▾

Both the patient and prescribing provider receive an alert. The patient sees a clear explanation: "Your new sulfa allergy may conflict with sulfasalazine." The provider sees clinical details.

How This Helps
  • Dual notification ensures both parties are aware of the conflict
  • Patient-friendly language makes the alert understandable
  • Provider alert includes clinical context for decision-making
4
Drug Interaction Dashboard
Medications
▾

The patient views their complete interaction profile — all potential drug-drug interactions, drug-food interactions, and allergy cross-references in one dashboard. Risk levels are color-coded.

How This Helps
  • Comprehensive interaction view reveals the complete safety picture
  • Color-coded risk levels prioritize which interactions need attention
  • Food interaction warnings include practical dietary guidance
5
Provider Discussion & Resolution
Messaging
▾

The patient messages their provider about the alert. The provider reviews alternatives and switches the medication. The interaction dashboard updates to reflect the resolved conflict.

How This Helps
  • In-context messaging enables efficient resolution of alerts
  • Alternative medication suggestions speed provider decision-making
  • Real-time dashboard updates confirm resolution
