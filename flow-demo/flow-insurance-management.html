Insurance Card Upload & Verification

Managing insurance information with card scanning, real-time eligibility verification, coverage details, and prior authorization tracking.

Insurance Authorization Eligibility Coverage OCR Benefits
1
Card Upload & OCR
Insurance
▾

The patient photographs both sides of their new insurance card after changing employers. The system uses OCR to extract plan name, member ID, group number, and contact information.

How This Helps
  • Camera-based capture works on any smartphone
  • OCR extraction reduces manual entry errors
  • Both sides captured ensures complete information
2
Real-Time Eligibility Check
Insurance
▾

The system immediately queries the payer's eligibility system. Within seconds, the patient sees: active coverage confirmed, PCP assignment verified, copay amounts for different visit types.

How This Helps
  • Real-time verification eliminates eligibility surprises
  • Visit-type specific copays enable financial planning
  • PCP assignment confirmation prevents referral issues
3
Coverage Details Review
Insurance
▾

The patient explores their plan's coverage details — deductible progress, in-network vs. out-of-network benefits, covered services, and exclusions. The system highlights changes from their previous plan.

How This Helps
  • Side-by-side plan comparison highlights coverage changes
  • Deductible progress tracking supports healthcare spending decisions
  • Exclusion visibility prevents unexpected out-of-pocket costs
4
Prior Authorization Tracker
Authorization
▾

The patient has a pending MRI that requires prior authorization. The system shows the authorization status: submitted by the provider, under review by insurance, with an estimated decision timeline.

How This Helps
  • Authorization status transparency reduces anxiety and phone calls
  • Estimated timelines help patients plan around procedures
  • Denial notifications include next steps and appeal options
5
Coordination of Benefits
Insurance
▾

The patient has dual coverage (employer + spouse's plan). The system manages coordination of benefits — showing which plan is primary, how claims are split, and the combined coverage picture.

How This Helps
  • Automatic COB determination simplifies dual coverage
  • Combined coverage view shows total out-of-pocket picture
  • Proper primary/secondary assignment prevents claim rejections
