Flow 06 of 15

Visit Summary & After-Visit Instructions

Post-visit documentation delivery with diagnosis details, treatment plans, care instructions, and automatic health record updates.

Visit Summaries Health Records Care Plans Scheduling Notifications Patient Education
1
Summary Generation
Visit Summaries
▾

Within minutes of the visit ending, the system generates a patient-friendly visit summary from the provider's clinical notes. Medical jargon is translated into plain language.

How This Helps
  • Plain language translation improves patient understanding
  • Automatic generation ensures timely delivery
  • Provider-reviewed summaries maintain clinical accuracy
2
Diagnosis & Treatment Plan
Health Records
▾

The patient reviews their diagnosis (contact dermatitis), prescribed treatments, and lifestyle modifications. Each item includes educational links and resources.

How This Helps
  • Educational resources deepen patient understanding of their condition
  • Treatment plans with clear steps improve adherence
  • Linked resources reduce unnecessary follow-up questions
3
After-Visit Instructions
Care Plans
▾

Detailed care instructions are provided — wound care steps, medication schedules, warning signs to watch for, and when to seek emergency care. Instructions can be printed or saved.

How This Helps
  • Step-by-step instructions prevent confusion at home
  • Warning sign checklists help patients know when to call
  • Printable format serves patients who prefer paper
4
Health Record Update
Health Records
▾

The visit automatically updates the patient's health record — new allergies, updated medication list, and condition history. Changes are highlighted in the patient timeline.

How This Helps
  • Automatic record updates eliminate manual data entry
  • Change highlighting makes updates easy to spot
  • Longitudinal timeline shows health journey over time
5
Follow-Up Scheduling
Scheduling
▾

Based on the care plan, the system suggests a follow-up visit in 2 weeks. The patient can book immediately or set a reminder. Automated check-ins track symptom progress between visits.

How This Helps
  • Proactive follow-up suggestions improve care continuity
  • Between-visit check-ins catch complications early
  • Reminder scheduling prevents follow-ups from falling through the cracks
