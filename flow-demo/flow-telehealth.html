Telehealth Visit

End-to-end virtual care experience from pre-visit setup through video consultation to post-visit documentation and prescriptions.

Telehealth Prescriptions Visit Summaries Scheduling Notifications Pharmacy
1
Pre-Visit Tech Check
Telehealth
▾

Fifteen minutes before the telehealth visit, the patient receives a link to test their camera, microphone, and internet connection. The system provides troubleshooting tips if issues are detected.

How This Helps
  • Pre-visit tech check prevents wasted appointment time
  • Automated troubleshooting reduces support calls
  • Bandwidth detection switches to audio-only if video is unstable
2
Virtual Waiting Room
Telehealth
▾

The patient joins the virtual waiting room and sees their position in the queue. Educational content about their condition plays while they wait. The provider can see all waiting patients.

How This Helps
  • Queue position visibility reduces patient anxiety
  • Educational content makes wait time productive
  • Provider dashboard enables flexible schedule management
3
Video Consultation
Telehealth
▾

The video visit begins with secure, HIPAA-compliant video. The patient can share their screen to show photos of skin conditions, and the provider can annotate images during the discussion.

How This Helps
  • Screen sharing enables visual examination remotely
  • Image annotation helps explain conditions to patients
  • End-to-end encryption protects all conversation content
4
Digital Prescription
Prescriptions
▾

The provider prescribes a topical cream through e-prescribing. The patient selects their preferred pharmacy from a list, and the prescription is sent electronically before the visit ends.

How This Helps
  • E-prescribing eliminates paper prescriptions and phone calls to pharmacies
  • Pharmacy selection gives patients control over pickup location
  • Formulary checking ensures the medication is covered by insurance
5
Visit Summary & Follow-Up
Visit Summaries
▾

After the visit, the patient receives a visit summary with diagnosis, treatment plan, and follow-up instructions. A follow-up appointment is suggested and can be booked immediately.

How This Helps
  • Immediate visit summary ensures patients remember key points
  • Treatment plan documentation creates accountability
  • One-click follow-up booking maintains continuity of care
