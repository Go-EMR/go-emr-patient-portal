Provider Search & Appointment Booking

Finding the right provider based on specialty, location, availability, and insurance acceptance — then booking an appointment with real-time calendar integration.

Provider Directory Scheduling Insurance Calendar Notifications Reviews
1
Search by Specialty & Location
Provider Directory
▾

The patient searches for a dermatologist within 10 miles. The system returns providers sorted by proximity, showing ratings, languages spoken, and next available slots.

How This Helps
  • Location-based search prioritizes convenience for the patient
  • Provider ratings help patients make informed choices
  • Insurance acceptance filtering eliminates surprise costs
2
View Provider Profile
Provider Directory
▾

The patient views Dr. Smith's profile — credentials, specialties, languages, office photos, and patient reviews. The profile includes a real-time availability calendar showing open slots.

How This Helps
  • Comprehensive profiles build patient confidence in their choice
  • Real-time availability eliminates phone calls to check schedules
  • Patient reviews provide peer perspectives on provider quality
3
Select Appointment Slot
Scheduling
▾

The patient selects a Wednesday 2 PM slot. The system shows the appointment type options (new patient visit, follow-up, telehealth) and estimated duration for each.

How This Helps
  • Appointment type selection ensures proper time allocation
  • Duration estimates help patients plan their day
  • Same-day and next-day filtering serves urgent needs
4
Insurance Verification
Insurance
▾

Before confirming, the system performs a real-time insurance eligibility check. The patient sees their copay amount, deductible status, and whether prior authorization is needed.

How This Helps
  • Pre-appointment eligibility prevents billing surprises
  • Copay transparency enables financial planning
  • Prior auth requirements are identified before the visit
5
Booking Confirmation
Scheduling
▾

The appointment is confirmed with a calendar invite sent via email and added to the patient's GoHealth calendar. Reminder preferences are set and pre-visit instructions are provided.

How This Helps
  • Calendar integration syncs with Google, Apple, and Outlook calendars
  • Customizable reminders reduce no-show rates by up to 60%
  • Pre-visit instructions prepare patients for their appointment
