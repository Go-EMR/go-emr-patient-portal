Annual Wellness Reminder Flow

Proactive health maintenance through personalized wellness reminders, preventive care scheduling, health risk assessments, and wellness goal tracking.

Notifications Forms Preventive Care Scheduling Wellness Goals Health Records
1
Wellness Reminder Trigger
Notifications
▾

One month before the patient's annual wellness visit is due, the system sends a personalized reminder. The reminder includes age-appropriate screening recommendations based on USPSTF guidelines.

How This Helps
  • Proactive reminders prevent care gaps between annual visits
  • USPSTF-based recommendations ensure evidence-based screening
  • Personalization based on age, sex, and risk factors improves relevance
2
Health Risk Assessment
Forms
▾

The patient completes an annual health risk assessment — questions about lifestyle, mental health, social determinants, family history updates, and current symptoms. The assessment generates a risk score.

How This Helps
  • Standardized HRA captures a holistic health picture
  • Social determinants screening identifies non-medical health barriers
  • Risk scoring prioritizes which screenings are most important
3
Preventive Care Checklist
Preventive Care
▾

Based on the HRA and the patient's profile, the system generates a personalized preventive care checklist — mammogram due, colonoscopy screening recommended, flu shot available, dental cleaning overdue.

How This Helps
  • Personalized checklists eliminate guesswork about what's needed
  • Multi-specialty coverage coordinates across providers
  • Status tracking shows what's been completed and what's outstanding
4
Book Recommended Screenings
Scheduling
▾

The patient books their mammogram and flu shot directly from the checklist. The system finds available slots at in-network facilities and handles pre-authorization if required.

How This Helps
  • One-click booking from the checklist reduces scheduling friction
  • In-network facility filtering prevents out-of-pocket surprises
  • Pre-authorization handling removes a common scheduling barrier
5
Wellness Goal Setting
Wellness Goals
▾

The patient sets health goals for the coming year — lose 15 pounds, walk 10,000 steps daily, reduce screen time. The system tracks progress and sends encouraging notifications.

How This Helps
  • Goal setting creates accountability for health improvement
  • Progress tracking provides motivation through visible results
  • Encouraging notifications reinforce positive behavior changes
6
Annual Wellness Report
Health Records
▾

At the wellness visit, the provider reviews the patient's annual wellness report — HRA results, completed screenings, goal progress, and year-over-year health trend comparisons.

How This Helps
  • Comprehensive report saves provider time during the visit
  • Year-over-year comparison reveals health trajectory
  • Patient preparation makes the wellness visit more productive
